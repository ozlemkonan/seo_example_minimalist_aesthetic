Do You Need Covid Safe Travel Ideas?

Would you like to wake up in a Luxurious Airstream at Russian, River?

I promised to give you more details about my covid safe trip last week, also I think it may be helpful if you plan to stay somewhere safe along the long weekend. By the way, this is not a sponsored post, I just want to share my experience there.

As I mentioned before that my close friend Ozlem Konan and I decided to make a few days trip to Russian River for her birthday celebration two weeks ago. We were careful and responsibly drove about 1.5 hours to the Russian River, California. We stayed at Autocamp which located in a grove of native Redwood trees. Property surrounded by a fence and a secure gate. It was a contactless check-in and check-out. There are luxurious canvas tents and Airstreams that all suites outfitted with luxurious bedding, linens and towels. Each of them their own private deck, campfire, and seats. Airstreams have 4 people of family capacity. They allow dogs at Airstreams. They have a shop that you can buy snacks, wine & beer, or camping essentials at their Mid-century modern architectural clubhouse. Their location is perfect for walking or riding a bike to the River and downtown Guerneville. Also great location to hike in the Redwoods.

Hope this quick information helps, please contact me, if you have any questions about our stay.
